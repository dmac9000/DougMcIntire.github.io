August 16, 2004

I passed the Microsoft Windows 2000 Server Exam

A passing score of 700 was required for Exam 070-215, Installing, Configuring, and Administering Microsoft Windows 2000 Server. I score 902 points. This made me a Microsoft Certified Professional, again.

July 28, 2004

I passed the Microsoft Windows 2000 Professional Exam

A score of 700 points was required to pass Exam 070-210, Installing, Configuring, and Administering Microsoft Windows 2000 Professional. I scored of 906 points. This made me a Microsoft Certified Professional, again.

March 15, 2004

CompTIA Network+ Certified

I passed the CompTIA Network+ Certification today. I scored 833 out of 900 points. I Needed 646 points to pass.

I went on to train Network+ Certification classes at a company called Job Ready Training.

February 20, 2004

I became CompTIA A+ Certified

I completed the A+ Operating Systems Technologies Exam. A passing score 505 was required. My score was 604. The completion of this exam made me A+ Certified.

I passed the CompTIA A+ Core Hardware Exam on February 11, 2004. It was the first of the two exams necessary to become A+ Certified. A passing score of 515 was required for the Core Hardware Examination. I scored 554.

I went on to train A+ Certification classes at a company called Job Ready Training.

May 25, 2001

Bronze Certificates for Two Homebrew Craft Beers

For a short time, I made homemade beer, otherwise known as "Homebrew". In 2001, I entered some of my beers into the American Homebrewers Association National Homebrew Competition. Two of my beers won bronze in the competition.

1998

Michael (Dell) Says I Know The Net

I've included this because I find it especially funny. The person who wrote the training and the test didn't know anything about networking or "the net", and relied heavily on me and another person, for her information. In short, we basically wrote the training and the test for her.

When it was all said and done the two of us, who actually wrote the training and the test, got in trouble because we hadn't completed this "mandatory" training requirement. The irony of it all made me save this certificate, and when I see it, I chuckle, because "I Know the Net".
