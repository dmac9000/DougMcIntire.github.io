Accomplishments page 3

November 2005

I Graduated with my Master of Arts Degree, Adult Education

I attended the University of Phoenix, Online, to receive my Master of Arts Degree in Adult Eductation and Distance Learning. I found it somewhat ironic to receive a degree in distance learning through distance learning.

I started in May 2005. I graduated with 33 Semester Hours and a GPA of 3.97.

January 27, 2005

I became a Microsoft Certified Desktop Support Technician (MCDST)

A passing score of 700 was required. My score was 838. This was the second test, which made me a Microsoft Certified Desktop Support Technician for Windows XP. I was one of the first 5,000 certified in the world, which made me a Charter Member. The Test ID was 070-272.

I passed the first of the two certifications required on January 17, 2005. A passing score of 700 was required. My score was 813. The Test ID was 070-271.

I went on to train this certification at a company called Job Ready Training.

September 27, 2004

I Passed the Microsoft Windows Server 2003 Certification

The passing score required was 700 points for Managing and Maintaining a Microsoft Windows Server 2003 Environment, Exam 070-290. I scored 895 points, making me a Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), again.

September 18, 2004

I became CompTIA i-Net+ Certified

The CompTIA i-Net+ was a relatively new certification. It demonstrated a basic understanding of networking concepts and principles. A score of 655 points was required. I scored 833 points out of 900 possible. This certification has since been retired.

September 13, 2004

I Passed the Microsoft Windows XP Certification

A passing score of 700 was requred for Exam 070-270, Installing, Configuring and Administering Microsoft Windows XP Professional. I scored 938 points, and became a Microsoft Certified Professional, again.

August 30, 2004

I Became a Windows 2000 Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA)

By completing Exam 070-218, Managing a Microsoft Windows 2000 Network Environment, along with the previous two exams (070-210 and 070-215), I became a Windows 2000 Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator (MCSA). This exam required a passing score of 700 points. I scored 832 points.
