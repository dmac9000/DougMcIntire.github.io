September 2015

Austin Community College (ACC)

Much of my work has been for Dell & Netgate, and is under NDA or for internal use only, so I am limited on what I can share, but in September 2015, I interviewed for a position at ACC in the Adult Education Department. They asked me to prepare a short sample of training to present during my interview. I chose to do the training on Geometry, and I wrote an Instructor Guide and a Student Guide for those who were interviewing me. It was a great experience, and I was hired on the spot, a first in my career.

February 2004

Transition Tech Lab

In February 2004, I did some volunteer training for a non-profit orginization called Transition Tech Labs. The training was designed to help unemployed individuals learn new computer skills. I developed a class to teach HTML coding for website creation.

I taught a similar HTML night class for adults at Reagan High School (now Northeast High School) in Austin in 2004 using some of the same material I developed.

Note: Some of what I taught then has now been depricated in HTML5 and Style is now used to accomplish similar tasks.
