Accomplishments page 2

November 2007

National Novel Writing Month (NaNoWriMo) — 2007

This year I wrote a novel titled The Second Passover, a story about a normal high school kid who wakes up one day and discovers he is all alone. I finished on November 23rd with 53,030 words.

Note: This was my fifth novel!

November 15, 2007

"In Recognition for their contribution and dedication with ACI Assessements and New Hire 1.5."

This wasn't the first, or the last, award I received from Dell, but most didn't have dates associated with them.

August 11, 2007

A New Witch — A Novel by Doug McIntire

On Friday the 13th, July 2007, I started working on A New Witch, a novel about Edie, a twelve-year-old girl who acquires an old and powerful Book of Shadows, the instruction book for a witch. This is by far my favorite of all the novels I've written.

I wrote it in NaNoWriMo style, finishing it within 30 days. I completed it on August 11, 2007 with 54,460 words.

Note: This was my fourth novel!

June 9, 2007

The Making of a King — A Novel by Doug McIntire

I started working on The Making of a King, a novel about a race of aliens that are monitoring the technological progress of Earth, hoping that we will accidentally stumble upon a cure for a condition that is eradicating their race.

I wrote it in NaNoWriMo style, finishing it within 30 days. I completed it on June 9, 2007 with 62,155 words.

Note: This was my third novel!

January 17, 2007

Erasure — A Novel by Doug McIntire

After I finished my first novel before the end of November 2006, I started a second one I titled Erasure. I finished it on January 17, 2007 with 86,306 words

Note: This was my second novel!

November 2006

National Novel Writing Month (NaNoWriMo) — 2006

During the month of November, people from all over the world each work to write a novel before the month is over. A novel, in this endeavor, is considered 50,000 words, which breaks down to 1,667 words a day. This was my first attempt, and I succeeded.

The book was titled The Life of Larry, a story about a group of college kids who discover a nest of aliens. I finished it on November 26, 2006, with 72,024 words.

Note: While all my novels remained unpublished, it was a wonderful accomplishment to have written my first novel!
